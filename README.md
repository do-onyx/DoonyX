# DoonyX

[DoonyX](https://doonyx.com) is a free online tools hub, It has  many cross-domain tools for every need. From business to education, finance to health - solve your daily problem.

Since **DoonyX** is not fully open-source, this repository serves as a place for bug reports, feature requests, and general discussion.

---

## Supported Tools


### Finance

1. [Mortgage Calculator](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/finance/mortgage-calculator): Calculate monthly payments, total interest, and amortization schedules for home loans with customizable terms and rates.

2. [Time Value of Money Calculator](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/finance/tvm-calculator): Solve for any TVM variable: PV, FV, PMT, I/YR, or N for loans and investments. Essential tool for financial planning based on the principle that money available today is worth more than the same amount in the future.

3. [Depreciation Calculator](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/finance/depreciation-calculator): Calculate asset depreciation for tax and accounting using multiple methods including Straight-Line, Declining Balance, Sum-of-Years-Digits, and MACRS.

4. [Debt to Income Ratio Calculator](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/finance/debt-to-income-calculator): Assess financial health with debt-to-income ratio calculation. Track all income sources and debt obligations to get instant DTI analysis with personalized recommendations.

5. [Debt Payoff Planner](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/finance/plan/debt-payoff-planner): Plan your debt payoff with detailed amortization schedules, interest savings comparisons, and payoff timeline visualization using avalanche or snowball methods.

6. [Emergency Fund Calculator](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/finance/emergency-fund-calculator): Determine emergency fund targets, create savings plans, and assess desperation financing risks with personalized recommendations. Calculate your ideal emergency fund based on monthly expenses, employment type, and financial risk factors.

7. [Personal Retained Earnings Calculator](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/finance/personal-retained-earnings-calculator): Calculate savings rate, track cumulative contributions, and assess financial discipline. Track your savings performance and measure your ability to consistently generate and save money.

### Health

1. [BMI Calculator](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/health/bmi-calculator): Check if your weight is in the healthy range for your height. Calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI): and estimate body fat percentage with support for both metric and imperial unit 

2. [Calorie Calculator](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/health/calorie-calculator): Determine your daily calorie requirements for weight loss, gain, or maintenance. Calculate daily caloric needs based on age, weight, height, and activity level using proven formulas like Mifflin-St Jeor and Harris-Benedict equations with personalized macronutrient recommendations.

### Education

1. [GPA Calculator](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/education/gpa-calculator/): Compute your current or projected GPA with detailed course breakdown and semester tracking. Calculate your Grade Point Average based on course grades and credit hours with support for weighted and unweighted calculations.

### Business

1. [Salary Converter](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/business/salary-converter): Convert salary between hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, and annual pay periods. Supports different pay periods including hourly, daily, weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, monthly, quarterly, and annual rates with accounting for holidays and vacation days.

2. [Break-even Calculator](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/business/break-even-calculator/): Find when your business will start making profit with break-even analysis. Determine the point at which your business becomes profitable by calculating when revenue equals total costs with analysis of fixed costs, variable costs, contribution margins, and profit scenarios.

### Conversion

1. [Unit Conversion Calculator](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/conversion/unit-conversion/): Convert between metric and imperial units instantly. Convert between different units of measurement for length, temperature, area, volume, and weight with real-time conversions.

### Utilities

1. [QR Code Generator](https://doonyx.com/en/tools/utilities/qr-code-generator): Create QR codes for various content types with custom styling. Generate customizable QR codes for URLs, text, contact cards, WiFi credentials, email, and phone numbers. Download as PNG images with adjustable size, colors, and error correction.
